Am I in Perimenopause? Free Checker

Navigating the transition into perimenopause can be confusing, especially when symptoms overlap with everyday stress and aging. If you're over 35 and noticing changes in your body, mood, or menstrual cycle, you might be wondering: 'Am I in perimenopause?'

Our Free Perimenopause Checker is a comprehensive tool designed to help you identify the early signs of this natural life stage. Perimenopause can begin years before your final period, bringing a host of symptoms such as irregular cycles, hot flashes, night sweats, unexplained weight gain, and mood swings.

Because these changes happen gradually, they are often dismissed or misdiagnosed. Our checker evaluates your specific symptoms and age to provide a clear indication of whether you might be entering the perimenopausal transition. Armed with this knowledge, you can have more informed conversations with your healthcare provider and start implementing proactive lifestyle changes.
